Toploader Support

Toploader is the easiest way to track what you own and what you sold it for — scan slabs, log purchases and sales, and see market values for your graded cards.

Common questions

  • Where do value estimates come from? Recent completed sales of the exact card and grade, gathered from public market sources. They're estimates, not appraisals, and refresh about once a day.
  • A value looks wrong. Tap the refresh arrow on the card's Estimated Value section, and email us the card if it still looks off.
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Toploader Premium.
  • How do I delete my account? Email us and we'll remove your account and data.
